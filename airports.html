Our Airports

GACL manages airports across Ghana

Kotoka International Airport

Location: Accra, Greater Accra Region

Type: International Airport

Passengers: 3.5 million annually

Runways: 2 (3,400m & 2,438m)

Ghana's main international gateway and the busiest airport in the country.

Kumasi Airport

Location: Kumasi, Ashanti Region

Type: Domestic/Regional

Passengers: 500,000 annually

Runways: 1 (1,981m)

Serving the Ashanti Region and surrounding areas with domestic and regional flights.

Tamale Airport

Location: Tamale, Northern Region

Type: Domestic/Regional

Passengers: 300,000 annually

Runways: 1 (2,438m)

Connecting the Northern Region to the rest of Ghana and West Africa.

Sunyani Airport

Location: Sunyani, Bono Region

Type: Domestic

Passengers: 150,000 annually

Runways: 1 (1,400m)

Serving the Bono Region with domestic flight connections.

Ho Airport

Location: Ho, Volta Region

Type: Domestic

Passengers: 100,000 annually

Runways: 1 (1,200m)

Providing air connectivity to the Volta Region of Ghana.

Takoradi Airport

Location: Takoradi, Western Region

Type: Domestic

Passengers: 80,000 annually

Runways: 1 (1,200m)

Serving the Western Region with domestic flight services.
