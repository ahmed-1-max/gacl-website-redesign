Our Story

Ghana Airports Company Limited (GACL) was established in 2006 to manage, operate, and maintain all airports in Ghana. With over 15 years of experience, we have grown to become a leading aviation company in West Africa.

Our mission is to provide world-class airport services and facilities that enhance the travel experience while contributing to Ghana's economic development.
